Based on this definition, and regardless of your gender, have you personally experienced any of the following in the last year because of your gender?

  • Physical Violence: Being beaten, slapped, pushed or restrained.
  • Sexual Violence: Upskirting, unwanted touching, being coerced into sexual acts.

What does this mean?

Questions relating to gender-based violence have been included in the Young Life and Times (YLT) survey since 2022 to attempt to gain more understanding of the prevalence of gender-based violence in Northern Ireland.

The YLT is a representative survey of 16-year-olds in Northern Ireland, and respondents are provided with definitions of gender-based violence. The data from this survey represents self-reported experience of gender-based violence.

After initial question development, a time series will be developed in future years from a baseline in 2023. Economic violence was not asked in the YLT survey due to the age of respondents.
